# Code Of Conduct

POKROV should be a calm and practical project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but stay precise.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work.
- Protect user privacy and security.
- Avoid public speculation about private infrastructure or users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- publishing secrets, tokens, private connection links, or personal data
- impersonating official POKROV releases or support
- encouraging abuse of the service or third-party networks
- repeatedly derailing issues or reviews

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments and issues that make the
project less safe or harder to maintain.
